# HR-Analysis-Dashboard | Employee Performance & Workforce Insights
This Repo contains an end-to-end analysis on Employees performance in Meridian Financial Services
## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an interactive Human Resources (HR) Analytics Dashboard developed to transform employee data into actionable business insights. The dashboard enables HR professionals and organizational leaders to monitor workforce performance, compensation trends, absenteeism, and employee distribution across departments and business locations.

The objective of this project is to demonstrate how data analytics can support strategic human resource management, improve workforce planning, and drive informed business decisions.


## 🎯 Business Problem

Human Resource departments often struggle to gain a comprehensive view of employee performance, compensation, and workforce efficiency. Without effective reporting, organizations may face challenges such as:

- Unequal salary distribution
- High absenteeism
- Underperforming departments
- Workforce planning inefficiencies
- Difficulty identifying high-performing teams
- Limited visibility into payroll allocation across locations

## 📊 Key Business Insights

The dashboard enables stakeholders to answer important business questions such as:

- Which department has the highest average employee performance?
- Which employment type receives the highest average salary?
- Which business location has the highest payroll cost?
- Which departments experience the highest absenteeism?
- How does salary differ across departments and locations?
- How is the workforce distributed across employment types?
- Which locations require workforce optimization?


## 💡 Analytical Insights

Some notable insights generated from the dashboard include:

- Comparison of average salaries across departments and employment types.
- Identification of payroll concentration across business locations.
- Analysis of workforce distribution by department and employment category.
- Evaluation of absenteeism trends to identify productivity risks.
- Performance comparison across organizational units. This dashboard addresses these challenges by providing clear, interactive visualizations and key performance indicators.
